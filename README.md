# Wonderland Explorer

This is my first Discord bot that actually made to public production. The rest was scraps that never end up finishing.

This is Wonderland Explorer! Your companion for sharing Genshin Impact - Miliastra Wonderland Stages!

How it works: When you like the wonderland you play and want to share with your friends, you can type or paste the GUID of the level to the bot and it will show the full details of the level!

Your friends can then view the level on HoYoLAB or even hop directly into Genshin Impact or Genshin Impact - Cloud and experience the level!*

[Install App](https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1441690798293909534)

![Showcase](./img/Wonderland_Explorer_Showcase.gif)

* Note: Ability to hop into the game requires user to have Genshin Impact (and by extension HoYoPlay Launcher on PC) or Genshin Impact - Cloud installed for the ability to work. Third-party Cloud Launchers does not work.
